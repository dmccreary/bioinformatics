# References: Metabolic Pathway Modeling

1. [Metabolic Network](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Metabolic_network) - Wikipedia - Overview of metabolic networks as graphs of biochemical reactions, covering stoichiometric matrices, network topology, and the relationship between metabolic structure and cellular function.

2. [Flux Balance Analysis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Flux_balance_analysis) - Wikipedia - Explains the constraint-based modeling approach using stoichiometric matrices and linear programming to predict metabolic flux distributions under steady-state assumptions and growth optimization.

3. [Metabolic Pathway](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Metabolic_pathway) - Wikipedia - Describes the organization of enzyme-catalyzed reactions into pathways, covering glycolysis, citric acid cycle, and oxidative phosphorylation as examples of core metabolic network modules.

4. Systems Biology: Constraint-Based Reconstruction and Analysis - Bernhard Palsson - Cambridge University Press - Definitive textbook on constraint-based metabolic modeling covering stoichiometric matrices, flux balance analysis, and genome-scale metabolic model reconstruction.

5. Metabolic Engineering: Principles and Methodologies - Gregory Stephanopoulos - Academic Press - Comprehensive treatment of metabolic pathway analysis, flux quantification, and engineering strategies, providing theoretical foundations for computational pathway modeling.

6. [KEGG Pathway Database](https://www.kegg.jp/kegg/pathway.html) - KEGG - Documentation for browsing and accessing KEGG metabolic pathway maps, covering pathway hierarchy, organism-specific maps, and programmatic access via the KEGG REST API.

7. [COBRApy Documentation](https://cobrapy.readthedocs.io/en/latest/) - openCOBRA - Official documentation for the Python package for constraint-based reconstruction and analysis, covering model creation, flux balance analysis, gene knockouts, and flux variability analysis.

8. [BiGG Models Database](http://bigg.ucsd.edu/about) - UCSD Systems Biology - Documentation for the BiGG database of genome-scale metabolic network reconstructions, providing standardized models for flux balance analysis and constraint-based modeling.

9. [Reactome Pathway Analysis Tools](https://reactome.org/PathwayBrowser/) - Reactome - Guide to the Reactome pathway browser and analysis service for exploring metabolic and signaling pathways, performing enrichment analysis, and visualizing pathway data.

10. [MetaNetX](https://www.metanetx.org/mnxdoc/help.html) - MetaNetX/SIB - Documentation for the MetaNetX resource that reconciles metabolite and reaction identifiers across databases, enabling integration of metabolic network data from multiple sources.
