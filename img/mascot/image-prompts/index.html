    Olli the Octopus — Image Generation Prompts

    Design Notes (for consistency across all 7 images)

    • Character: Olli the Octopus
    • Color: Teal body (soft, not neon), slightly lighter underside
    • Face: Large kind eyes, small round glasses, gentle smile
    • Arms: 8 arms, subtly arranged to hint at graph/network structure
    • Style: Flat vector, clean lines, minimal shading
    • Background: Fully transparent (PNG alpha channel)
    • Framing: Compact, centered, icon-friendly

    Base Prompt

    I am about to ask you to generate seven PNG images of a mascot used for a bioinformatics course with transparent backgrounds.

    A flat cartoon illustration of Olli the Octopus, a friendly pedagogical mascot for a bioinformatics textbook. Olli is a round, soft teal octopus (not neon) with a slightly lighter underside. Olli has large, kind eyes with small round glasses perched on the face, and a gentle smile. Olli has eight arms subtly arranged to hint at graph and network connections. The character is small, compact, and centered in the frame, suitable for icon-sized display. Style: modern flat vector, clean lines, minimal shading, no outlines heavier than 2px. Fully transparent background (PNG alpha channel). No text in the image. No background color — not white, not black, not any color.

    Make sure to always use a transparent background. Never create a white or black background.
